Film Collaborations

Film Collaborations

The Bling Ring

In 2013, Lopatin composed the soundtrack for Sofia Coppola's The Bling Ring, being the the first film score he composed.

Partisan

He composed the score for the 2015 film Partisan, directed by Ariel Kleiman.

Good Time

Lopatin provided the soundtrack for the film Good Time, directed by Ben & Josh Safdie in 2017. He won the Soundtrack Award at the 2017 Cannes Film Festival for his work on the film, which included a collaboration with singer Iggy Pop entitled "The Pure and The Damned". The film's soundtrack was released via Warp on August 11, 2017.

Uncut Gems

In 2019, he composed the original score to the Safdie Brothers' feature film Uncut Gems.
